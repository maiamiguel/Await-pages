Await Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

Await respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how that information is used.

Information Await Stores

Await stores the countdown date you choose so the app and widget can display your countdown correctly.

  • Your selected target date is stored locally on your device.
  • This date may also be shared locally with the Await widget using Apple's app group storage so the widget can display the same countdown.

Information Await Does Not Collect

Await does not currently collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with the developer or third parties.

  • No account creation
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No location data collection
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access

How Your Information Is Used

The selected countdown date is used only to provide the app's core functionality, including showing your countdown in the app and in the widget.

Data Sharing

Await does not share your information with third parties.

Data Retention

Your selected countdown date remains on your device until you change it or remove the app.

Your Choices

You can update your countdown date at any time in the app. If you want to remove the stored data completely, you can delete the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Await does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
